Privacy Policy

Ponkit Inc. · Effective July 23, 2026

OttO is built on a simple promise: your notes never leave your iPhone. Recording, transcription, summaries, and chat all run on your device. OttO has no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

What OttO stores — on your device only

Your recordings, transcripts, summaries, action items, chats, folders, tags, and settings live only in the app's private storage on your iPhone. We cannot see, access, or recover them. Deleting the app deletes them. Use the share and export features to back up anything you want to keep.

What leaves your device

  • Nothing about your notes — ever. Audio, transcripts, and chats are never uploaded.
  • Optional AI model downloads. If you download a speech, voice, or chat model, the app fetches the model file from Hugging Face's public servers. This is a plain download; none of your content is sent.
  • Subscription processing. Purchases go through Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to validate subscriptions; it receives your App Store purchase receipt and a random, anonymous identifier — never your name, email, or notes. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Microphone and speech

OttO requests microphone access solely to record your notes and speech recognition solely to transcribe them, using Apple's on-device speech frameworks or a locally downloaded Whisper model. Recordings are processed and stored entirely on your device.

Children

OttO is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because OttO collects no personal data, changes are expected to be rare and minor.
