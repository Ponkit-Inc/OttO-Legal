OttO is a private, on-device AI note taker for iPhone. Recording, transcription, summaries, and chat all happen locally — your notes never leave your device.

Frequently asked

  • Where are my notes? Stored only on your iPhone. Use the share button in any note to export audio, text, or Markdown.
  • Transcription isn't working. Make sure OttO has microphone permission in iOS Settings, or download a Whisper model from the Models tab for fully offline transcription.
  • How do I get a more natural reading voice? Settings → Reading voice → "Get a Premium voice", then download a Premium voice in iOS Settings. It appears in OttO automatically.
  • Managing your subscription. OttO Pro renews through the App Store. Manage or cancel in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
